About TypeArcade

TypeArcade was built with a single mission: to make typing practice fun and colourful again. We were tired of the boring, dark, corporate-looking typing tests, so we built something that brings the arcade energy straight to your keyboard.

What is WPM?

Words Per Minute (WPM) measures your typing speed, usually calculated by dividing total correct characters by 5, over a minute interval.
