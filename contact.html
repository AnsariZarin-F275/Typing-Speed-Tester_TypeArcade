Frequently Asked Questions

How is accuracy calculated? +

Accuracy is determined by subtracting your missed keystrokes from your total keys typed, divided by total keys typed.

Are sounds mandatory? +

No! By default they are enabled once you interact, but you can globally turn them off in the Settings page.

Can I practice my code syntax? +

Currently we focus on common English words, but code syntax practice modes are coming soon!
